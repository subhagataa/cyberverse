SECURITY LESSON
QR Code Safety at Restaurants
❌ WHAT'S DANGEROUS
  • Scanning a QR code from a printed menu or table sticker without checking what it opens
  • Installing unknown apps just to "view a menu" — apps can request access to SMS, contacts, and camera
  • Entering your UPI PIN on any page that opens from an unknown QR
  • Trusting a QR code just because it looks official or is placed on a branded item
✅ WHAT'S SAFE
  • Ask the waiter for a physical menu or to bring their official payment QR directly to you
  • Pay in cash when unsure — it is always zero-risk
  • After scanning any QR, verify the "Pay to:" name matches the restaurant before entering your PIN
  • Never install an app from a QR code in a public place
💡 Rule of thumb: A QR code on a menu or table is a scammer's easiest target.
When in doubt — ask a human, not a QR code.
